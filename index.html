At its core, Palantir is a leader in big data analytics. The company’s flagship platforms, Palantir Foundry and Palantir Gotham, are used by governments and large corporations alike to analyze and make sense of vast amounts of data.

Although Palantir started out as a government contractor, it’s now making a major push into the commercial sector. This shift means that Palantir is not just dependent on government contracts anymore.
